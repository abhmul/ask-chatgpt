## Context — the bug (GAP-15, from M-006)
On a NEW CDP chat, the tool samples `conversation_ref` too early — in `send_prompt`, BEFORE the URL settles to `https://chatgpt.com/c/<id>` — so an EMPTY ref persists to the session registry and continuity can't resolve the same conversation later. **Fix:** AFTER completion is detected, RE-READ the settled conversation URL, derive the ref from it, and use THAT settled ref for the registry `set()` — for BOTH the text path and the patch-bundle path. Keep fail-closed: if the URL never settles to `/c/<id>`, do NOT fabricate or persist an empty/garbage ref.
